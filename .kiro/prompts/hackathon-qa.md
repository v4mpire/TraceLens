## Risk Assessment

### High Risk Items
- **Missing Demo Video**: Major presentation component (-3 points)
- **Time Constraints**: Limited time for additional features

### Medium Risk Items
- **Complexity**: Platform complexity might overwhelm judges
- **Technical Depth**: May be too technical for some evaluators

### Low Risk Items
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive and well-organized
- **Code Quality**: High standards maintained throughout
- **Innovation**: Clear differentiation from existing solutions

## Mitigation Strategies

### Critical Actions
1. **Create Demo Video**: 3-5 minute demonstration of key features
2. **Simplify Presentation**: Focus on core value proposition
3. **Highlight AI Integration**: Emphasize unique MCP server approach

### Enhancement Opportunities
1. **Performance Benchmarks**: Add concrete performance data
2. **User Testimonials**: Include developer feedback if available
3. **Comparison Matrix**: Show advantages vs existing tools
